JIS from the eyes of the parents

Mrs. Shipra Sabharwal

Parent of Viraj Singh Sabharwal (U.KG.)

I am Shipra Sabharwal, mother of Viraj Singh Sabarwal (U.KG-B).

The journey of JIS Kindergarten was amazing for us. The Management of JIS is very talented. They teach and take care of our little ones very smoothly. They understand each child to the best of his/her ability. My son's second home is JIS. He loves his school. Our little ones are spending a lot of time with all of you. Thank you for taking care of our children. We are blessed to have all of you in our lives. The teaching skills of JIS are amazing, easy way of learning, no burden on children and parents. Above all, our journey of JIS Kindergarten was full of fun. The canteen is also very hygienic and the food is prepared in a hygienic way. Food is healthy and tasty. Hats off to all of you.
My best wishes to the Management and staff of JIS , and a special thanks to Anmol Ma’am for being a part of our journey.

Mrs. Sara RiteshSachdeo

Parent of Neel Sachdeo (L.KG.)

One Morning at Jain International School…

My son Neel Sachdeo is studying in LKG class. From the last one year I have witnessed a lot of positive changes in my child. This has been possible because of the facilities provided by Jain International School.

  • Teaching and Non-Teaching Staff are very cooperative. Always available for kids &parents. No query is unsolved
  • Extra-Curricular activities have made the child competitive and stand different from others
  • Well structuredsyllabus which enhances the concepts step by step
  • Impressive teaching style, it's like play, visualize and learn
  • Well defined food menu for kids. Quality is primarily considered. My kid loves the food and everyday enquires what he will be getting the next day.
  • Spacious campus with good friendly environment
  • Organized transport administration

I am completely satisfied with the School, Staff, Administration, Transport facility.

Mrs Deepali Amit Sondeshkar

Parent of Kanishka Sondeshkar (I B)

After moving from Singapore, we were very much concerned about our daughter Kanishka. If she would be able to manage with the new school environment. The main hurdle was the language, as she was unable to understand and speak Hindi and Marathi.
We are very thankful to all the JIS teachers, co-ordinator and the supporting staff for their understanding, co-operation and efforts that they took for Kanishka. JIS is the one of the finest schools. The school has provided a superior platform for academics, sports and extra-curricular activities to everyone. Good hygiene practises are followed in classrooms as well as in canteen.
Its a matter of pride for us to have our child studying in JIS. Kanishka’s journey in Std I was memorable and she enjoyed it to the fullest extent.

Anurag & Pallavi Pashine

Parents of Aarmaan Pashine (II B)

Our son has been a part of Jain International school since 7 months. Since day one we have been impressed by the professionalism of the administration and the teaching staff. The teachers have all been extremely cooperative and accommodative. They have displayed the right balance of tenderness and discipline towards our child. We are proud to be associated with such an institute and we are sure our son will flourish under their able guidance.

Mr. Vishnu Tambi

Parent of Avni (VII) & Vedaant (VIII)

One Morning at Jain International School…

As a part of Value Education, I was invited to speak to students of the Primary and Secondary classes. The topic was “Hard work & Time Management”. As a parent, it was a rare opportunity to interact with such young students and influence their minds, which the teachers get to do every day with our children. I was impressed by the patience displayed by the students in attentively listening to what I had to say. A single meeting by a parent can influence them! Sounds farfetched, but my experience ssays – children learn by following the examples and role models in their lives, and regular reinforcement certainly shapes their lives.So, Value Education has a very important place in overall education system, and the efforts by JIS on this front shall be applauded. I hope that the school would do even more on this, given the general despair about ills & moral values plaguing our country & society. Also, I hope many more parents are invited, and that parents actively participate in this serious purpose of instilling the right values in the young and tender minds whether in school or at home.

Jai Hind,

Mrs. Swati Banerjee

Parent of Anika (II) & Rianna (I)

Sending my daughters, Anika and Rianna Banerjee to Jain International from this year was one of the best decisions I have made. To see my children get ready for school every morning with zest and vigor and to receive them from the bus with a smile on their face makes me a very happy parent. The education, ambience, the friendly, approachable, capable and confident teachers always ensure the children are taken care of and never fail to give individual attention to ensure all the children excel in every manner. It is an honour for me to be able to voice my thoughts in this issue of The Jewel.

Thank you and Best Wishes Always
